About Georgian Wine Heritage

Georgia is the birthplace of wine, with an unbroken tradition of winemaking that dates back 8,000 years. Our unique Qvevri method, recognized by UNESCO as Intangible Cultural Heritage, produces wines with distinctive character and complexity.
